Raena Frontend & UI/UX Internship

May 2021 – Aug 2021

Raena focuses on business-to-business (B2B) e-commerce, providing a platform for beauty resellers through its mobile app, primarily in the Indonesian market. During this 3-month internship, I worked on mobile app UI/UX design and website development for Raena. My responsibilities include identifying user experience problems, creating user interface (UI) mockups and prototypes, and developing websites.

Tasks

Website Revamps

I designed the 2 websites from scratch, following the discussions with the Product Managers.

Main Website

My aim was to better convey Raena’s mission, purpose & target audience. With the guidance of the team, I also made sure to put in emphasis on showing what business training resources Raena has for resellers.

Website is live at: raenabeauty.com

International Website

I redesigned and developed this website to fit Raena’s brand, and to be more organised and SEO-friendly. This website leverages Raena's good connections with credible suppliers, to reach a global audience.

Website is live at: raenabeautyintl.com

Tasks

Mobile App Interfaces

I worked with the Project Managers to identify and discuss user experience problems, and reviewed my user interface mockups with the UX strategist. I also learnt to design from a business and revenue perspective, making decisions based on the sales analysis and aiming for high conversion rate.

Brands Display

I designed a new Brands page for easier brand discovery and search by resellers. This gave me insight on the structure of e-commerce platforms' brand and product display.

App Gamification

I worked closely with the UX strategist to bring in a new gamification feature into the app. I designed this feature with the aim of increasing retention and encouraging user actions.

Tier Pricing

I was entrusted with the important task of designing a better representation of Raena's unique tier pricing system. I got the chance to discuss the design decisions with various stakeholders.
